About Us

We are a collective of the skinniest guys in our area. We banded together simply because we lacked the confidence to do what we wanted. We began aimlessly lifting weights and seeing little to no gains. THEN WE GOT SERIOUS. We began vigorous research and formulated a plan to get the physiques we always wanted. Four years later we wanted to create a platform to help guys like us.

We bring to you the skinny guy's fitness guide.
